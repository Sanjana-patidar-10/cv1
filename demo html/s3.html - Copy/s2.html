MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI

Dhoni was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now in Jharkhand), India.
He grew up in a middle-class family and had a keen interest in sports from a young age.
He began playing cricket as a schoolboy and quickly rose through the ranks to become a professional cricketer.
